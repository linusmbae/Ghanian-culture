Akan, ethnolinguistic grouping of peoples of the Guinea Coast who speak Akan languages (of the Kwa branch of the Niger-Congo family).
Most Akan peoples live in Ghana, where they settled in successive waves of migration between the 11th and 18th centuries;
others inhabit the eastern part of Côte d’Ivoire and parts of Togo.

In Ghana, children are given their first name as a "day-name" which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. This web application will help you,find out the day of the week you were born or someone was born.
It will also help determine to you your Akan Name, based on your gender and birthday month.
